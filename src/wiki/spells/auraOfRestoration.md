# Aura of Restoration

_Call upon the powers of death to reap the undead_

---

- **Casting Time:** 2 actions
- **Range:** Self
- **Components:** V, S, M (A red or pink coloured gem worth at least 1 gold)
- **Duration:** Concentration, Up to 10 minutes
