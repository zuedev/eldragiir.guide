Eldragiir

Birthed from embers and torn asunder by dragons, the world of Eldragiir is an ever-changing realm of mad-gods and foolish mortals. With everyone vying for control over precious artefacts left behind by a once-great ancient civilisation known only as The Precursors, much darker forces fester and scheme below the surface.
